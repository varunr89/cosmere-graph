# Coppermind:Editor's Corner

Source: https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Editor's_Corner

**The Coppermind has spoilers for all of Brandon's published works, now including *Isles of the Emberdark* and Cosmere RPG books. Information about books that have not yet been released, like *Mistborn* Era 3, is allowed *only* on meta-pages for the books themselves. For more details, see [our spoiler policy](/web/20260103193257/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Spoilers "Help:Spoilers"). To view an earlier version of the wiki without spoilers for a book, go to the [Time Machine](/web/20260103193257/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Special:TimeMachine "Special:TimeMachine")!**

Welcome to the Editor's Corner for the Coppermind! The goal with this page is to give some useful statistics on the state of the wiki, and also give new goals and objectives for editors. Also check out the [Coppermind Editors Discord](https://discord.gg/KEv3fhN) to communicate with editors and [Keepers](/web/20260103193257/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:Keepers "Coppermind:Keepers")!
