# Coppermind:About

Source: https://coppermind.net/wiki/Coppermind:About

**The Coppermind has spoilers for all of Brandon's published works, now including *Isles of the Emberdark* and Cosmere RPG books. Information about books that have not yet been released, like *Mistborn* Era 3, is allowed *only* on meta-pages for the books themselves. For more details, see [our spoiler policy](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Help:Spoilers "Help:Spoilers"). To view an earlier version of the wiki without spoilers for a book, go to the [Time Machine](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Special:TimeMachine "Special:TimeMachine")!**

**The Coppermind** is a comprehensive wiki about [Brandon Sanderson](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Brandon_Sanderson "Brandon Sanderson") and all of his work including the [The Stormlight Archive](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Stormlight_Archive "The Stormlight Archive") and [Mistborn series](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Mistborn_(series) "Mistborn (series)"), both of which are set in Brandon's connected universe, the [Cosmere](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Cosmere "Cosmere"). The Coppermind also includes information on Brandon's non-cosmere works, such as [Skyward](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Skyward_(series) "Skyward (series)"), [Reckoners](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/The_Reckoners "The Reckoners"), and [Alcatraz](/web/20251203054045/https://coppermind.net/wiki/Alcatraz_Versus_the_Evil_Librarians_(series) "Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians (series)"). It is owned and administrated by the [17th Shard](https://www.17thshard.com/).
